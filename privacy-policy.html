Privacy Policy for OpenClaw

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

OpenClaw is a personal AI assistant service operated by The Areharts. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with OpenClaw via SMS/text messaging.

2. Information We Collect

When you use OpenClaw, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Message content (texts you send to and receive from OpenClaw)
  • Timestamp of messages
  • Conversation history for context and functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide AI assistant services and respond to your messages
  • Maintain conversation context for better responses
  • Improve the assistant's functionality
  • Send reminders, updates, or information you've requested

4. Third-Party Services

OpenClaw uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic Claude AI: Processes message content to generate responses
  • Twilio: Delivers SMS messages

These services process your data according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share your personal information for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

Message history is retained to provide context for ongoing conversations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of messages at any time by texting STOP
  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Ask questions about how your data is used

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

OpenClaw is intended for family use. If used by minors, parental consent is required.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of OpenClaw constitutes acceptance of any changes.

10. Contact Information

For questions, concerns, or data requests, contact:
